Have patience with me, I shall soon have done.—She closed with this offer, resolving to break with me entirely, and wrote the next day to tell me that we never were to meet again.—Shefelttheengagementtobeasourceofrepentanceandmiserytoeach: shedissolvedit.—This letter reached me on the very morning of my poor aunt's death.
